The Draft

Your Path To The NAMC

Draft Day: To Be Announced — Los Angeles, California. The Free Agent Pool opens the day after Draft Day. Every rider not selected automatically enters the Free Agent Pool, and charter teams may sign Free Agents directly through roster lock, four weeks before Round 1. Your OVR rating is public. Your resume is your credential.

Inaugural Talent Exception — Years 1–3

Athletes exactly 18 years of age with verifiable, elite-level global racing resumes may bypass the Draft and enter as Free Agents. This exception expires permanently at the end of Year 3. Year 4 forward, the Draft is the only door.

Eligibility

18-Year Hard Floor

The minimum age for any NAMC professional is 18 years as of January 8, 2027. This is absolute. No exceptions. No developmental exemptions. Ever.
