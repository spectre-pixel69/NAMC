Who We Are

Built To Finish What The Sport Started

The North American Motocross Championship (NAMC) is a rider-first professional motorsport league — structured, premium, and disruptive. No rider pays to race. Compensation is guaranteed, transparent, and paid on time — always within 24 hours of Sunday's final Main Event. The league is sanctioned by the Chuckwalla Valley Motorcycle Association (CVMA) and operates under full CVMA safety and technical charters.

Rider-First

$0 entry fees for contracted professionals. Guaranteed salaries. Paid within 24 hours of the final Main Event.

Merit-Based

A fair, transparent, public draft. Every rider earns their seat — no factory handouts.

Fan-First

Free tickets in 2027. Full paddock access. The most affordable ticket in professional motorsports.

Fair Play

Professional officiating from the Sentinel corps. An 18-year hard age floor. No exceptions, ever.
