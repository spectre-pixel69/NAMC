Who They Are

The Official Sanctioning Body

The Chuckwalla Valley Motorcycle Association (CVMA) is the official sanctioning body for all NAMC events. The league operates under full CVMA safety and technical charters, and every round of the 20-round championship runs under CVMA-sanctioned officiating standards.

What CVMA Governs

Sentinel CertificationTrains and certifies the entire 24-member Sentinel officiating corps (CVMA Certified & CVMA Level 1).

Home Ground

Southern California Roots

The NAMC is based in Chuckwalla and Pala, Southern California. All NAMC charter agreements, satellite agreements, and league documents are governed by the laws of the State of California.
