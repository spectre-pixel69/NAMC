Team Partners

Manufacturers & Independent Teams

The championship is built for a balanced grid of 10 Manufacturer charters and 10 Independent Team charters. Manufacturers may enter Provisional charters in Year 1 or form satellite relationships under the NAMC Satellite Agreement Framework. Energy drink and stimulant beverage companies are permanently banned from all team names.
