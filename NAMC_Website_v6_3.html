Riders & Salary

$0 Entry Fee — Always
No rider in this league ever pays to compete. The old system charges $300 per event. We pay you.
$400K Min — 350 Class
Minimum guaranteed annual salary before a single gate drops. 250 Class: $200K. 125 & Women's: $100K.
Full Contract on Injury
Season-ending injury? Full contract paid immediately as a lump sum. No abandoned riders. Ever.
$1,000 Appearance Fee
Every rider earns $1,000 per round just for showing up. Guaranteed. 20 rounds = $20,000 minimum.
Charter System

20 Charters · Your Seat at the Table Is an Asset

Single Charter — S4 Four-Stroke
8 active riders across 4 classes. Factory teams receive preference. $800,000 round purse per event. Year One S4 only — Two-Stroke launches Year Two.
Charter as a Protected Asset
A charter is a legitimate business asset. Transfer, sell, or use as collateral subject to NAMC approval. The first 20 will always be the first 20.
25% Rider Revenue Pool
Minimum 25% of all NAMC gross league revenue — broadcast, digital, merch, sponsorships — distributed to all contracted riders at season end.
Zero Sanctioning Fees
$0 charged to any host facility. $75,000 TV Hosting Fee paid by NAMC to every track. Fan tickets free in 2027.
NAMC Development Series

Path to the Pros

Free to Enter. Always.
Registration costs nothing. Opens September 1, 2026. Ages 4–17. 24 classes. Glen Helen Raceway.
5-Step Ladder
Youth Foundation → Development → Competitive → Pro Pipeline → NAMC Rookie Draft. Every step defined.
Hard Rail: Age 17
Must turn 18 before March 27, 2027 to enter the Rookie Draft. $100K min salary. $25K Pro Debut Bonus.
