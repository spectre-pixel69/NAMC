The Founding Principle

Your Seat Is An Asset

In every other professional motocross series, your entry can be revoked. Your position is rented, not owned. In the NAMC, your charter is a protected, transferable ownership interest in the league — with real market value once it converts to permanent status.

Provisional & Satellite

Provisional charters are available to manufacturers only, for Year 1 only, in classes where they field a bike. Satellite agreements let a charter holder partner with a manufacturer or independent org while retaining full ownership — governed by the NAMC Satellite Agreement Framework and submitted to the Commission no later than 45 days before the declaration deadline.
